Voting Rules

In an online voting system, certain rules must be followed to ensure fairness, security, and transparency throughout the election process. Only registered and verified voters are allowed to participate, and each voter can cast their vote only once. Voters must log in using valid credentials, such as a username, password, or OTP, to prevent unauthorized access.

The voting must be conducted within the specified time period, and any votes cast after the deadline will not be counted. All votes are encrypted and stored securely to prevent tampering or duplication, and the system should maintain voter anonymity so that no one can trace a vote back to an individual.

Voting Rules

  1. Each registered voter can cast their vote only once.
  2. Voting credentials (email & password) must be kept confidential.
  3. Votes once submitted cannot be changed or withdrawn.
  4. Any attempt to manipulate or hack the system will lead to disqualification.
  5. The voting portal will remain open only during the official election period.
  6. Make sure your internet connection is stable before voting.
  7. Respect the privacy of other voters.
